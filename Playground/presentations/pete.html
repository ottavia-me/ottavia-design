What is Ottavia

Guided everyday
wellbeing choices

Ottavia is a proactive wellbeing system that integrates multi-domain data with human knowledge to deliver continuous, context-aware support for everyday wellbeing decisions.

The Problem

People have access to more health and performance information than ever before.

Books, podcasts, social media, wearables, training plans, blood tests, and AI have made knowledge widely available. Yet the same questions remain:

  • Am I eating enough for my training?
  • Should I focus on performance or weight loss right now?
  • How should I fuel before a race?
  • Is my sleep good enough for the way I'm pushing my body?
  • Am I recovering well enough?
  • What should I do today?

Who is it for

We serve people who care deeply about their health and performance.

  • People who train consistently and care about their health and performance.
  • They invest time in learning through books, podcasts, wearables, and experts.
  • They want to make better daily decisions, not consume more information.

Our mission is to bridge body signals with knowledge

There are two
interconnected modes in Ottavia

1 . Ottavia, the default. Reasons over user's data (sleep, training, nutrition, HRV, cycle, body comp) using its own knowledge.

2 . Ottavia + Experts, the same reasoning, deepened by an Expert you choose. The Expert provides the framework (their published work, protocols, decision logic). Ottavia applies it to your actual day. Same voice, deeper lens.

Experts side

  • Turn knowledge into personalized guidance at scale.
  • Books, methods, and frameworks become part of daily recommendations.
  • A new distribution and monetization channel.
  • Reach far beyond traditional coaching, consulting, or content.

Customer side

  • Access to the expertise you trust, applied to your own life.
  • Personal data combined with an expert's proven approach.
  • General advice turned into guidance that's relevant and contextual.
  • Actionable every day.

Images and references to experts are for demonstration purposes only. Featured individuals are not affiliated with Ottavia unless explicitly stated.

Safety and scope

Education, not medical advice.

  • Expert guidance shapes daily decisions, it never replaces medical care.
  • Users with clinical conditions are routed to their clinician, not Ottavia.
  • Every recommendation is attributed to its source, so users see exactly whose lens shaped it.
