Garmin Reconnect — Native iOS & Android

We're asking a favor. A bug on our side means we need the user to sign into Garmin one more time. The popup appears a few seconds after the app opens (not on cold launch — let them land on Home first). Native components only, empathetic and short.

Components used: iOS UIAlertController (alert + action sheet) · Android Material AlertDialog (text + single-choice) · Garmin-hosted OAuth webview · iOS banner / Material Snackbar for confirmation.
Tone: calm, reassuring, matter-of-fact. Frames the reconnect as an improvement we're making, not a problem they have to fix. The Historical Data toggle lives inside Garmin's own sign-in screen.
