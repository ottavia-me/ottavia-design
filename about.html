Some products are built to make you better.

Ottavia was built because we got tired of being told we needed to be.

Every wellness app was a leaderboard. Every score was a verdict. Every day, the same trade. Hand over your data, get back a number you couldn't act on.

We thought it could be different.

We started by asking what you actually want to know. Not what the data could be made to display.

Did I recover. Did I eat enough. Should I push or rest. Why am I tired.

The questions are simple. The answers require the system to think across everything at once.

So we built one that does.

Ottavia reads your sleep, your training, your meals, your mood, your cycle. Every signal, every day. She finds the thread between them. She does not score you. She speaks to you.

We are a small team. We care about what we make.
